The O'Schraves
 
  Julie Schryer (piano)

The O'Schraves have played for dances in the Algoma Ontario region for the last 8 years, as well as touring the US. After you've danced to their superb tune-playing and rock-solid groove, you'll rave about The O'Schraves!

 

Will Mentor
From Massachusetts via Vermont, just about our all-time favorite caller returns to Montreal. Travelling throughout North America and calling to hundreds in one room at events such as the Dance Flurry, Will is in high demand for both his contras and squares.
