An evening of New England contradances







Sassafras Stomp
Sweet, high-energy folk music from the Maine/Montana border!
  Johanna Davis (fiddle, voice)

"Drawing from New England, Quebec, Maritime and Old Time traditions, fiddler Johanna Davis and guitarist Adam Nordell weave harmony vocals and rhythmic accompaniment into their tunes."

" An engaging teacher who presents a wide variety of dances, including plenty of New England contras, squares, old chestnuts, and freshly minted dances."
