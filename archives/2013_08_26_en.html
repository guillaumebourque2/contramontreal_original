Christian Dawid

A clarinet virtuoso based in Berlin, renowned for his ground-breaking work in Yiddish and other east-Europe styles, Christian has played with Brave Old World, Budowitz, Frank London, and Theodore Bikel, and directs Konsonans Retro, the Ukrainian hipster brass band.
