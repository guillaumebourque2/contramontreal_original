Pete's Posse
 
  Pete Sutherland (fiddle, piano, banjo and vocals)  
  Oliver Scanlon (fiddle, mandolin, dobro, foot percussion and vocals)  
  Tristan Henderson (guitars, mandolin, jaw harp, bodhran, foot percussion and vocals)
 
Twin fiddles in tight, wild harmony over hi-powered guitar and pounding footwork with mandolin, clawhammer banjo, jawharp and rocking keyboards adding their voices to the mix. Old songs and tunes, lyrical and driving, along side brand new ones. That's the multi-generational heart and mind of Pete's Posse, at work and at play.


David Smukler
David is an expert caller, much loved by dancers for his wide range of calling skills, which ranges from historical dances to the latest in contra dances (or squares, or triplets, or mixers, or ...)! He is an expert on dance history and weaves knowledge into this calling that will delight. He also has a huge range of dances he has written too.
