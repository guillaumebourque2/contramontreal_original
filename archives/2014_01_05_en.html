Ayles, Kehler, Williams, & Stevens
  Mike Ayles (banjo, flute, guitar)
  Alex Kehler (fiddle, nyckelharpa)
  James Stephens (fiddle, string instruments)
  Nicholas Williams(accordion, flute, keyboard)
A power band comprising four of the top traditional musicians from the region playing together as a foursome for the first time. Including Nicholas Williams of Crowfoot, one of the great contra bands, Alex Kehler of the Baroque ensembles La Nef and Skye Consort, and James Stephens, the well-known Ottawa-based record producer.
