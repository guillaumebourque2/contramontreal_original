Contradancing With Style

A workshop led by Ric Goldman

Contradancing!  You’ve seen how fun, easy, and accessible this is.  With just a few minutes of introduction you’re swept up on the floor and enjoying a room of great folk, great music, and great energy.  You’ve picked it up on the fly.  You’ve got the basics, but now what about the next step?  Curious how to make dance movements flow?  Or how to interact better with your partner and the others in your dance set?  Wanna experience that moment of “ah ha!” when everything “clicks” together?  Well now’s your chance.  We’ll use this workshop to explore this and more.  You’ll come out of it more self-assured on the floor while enjoying everything about contradance even more. 

Join us for this special 2-hour session with caller Ric Goldman (California-based, but currently in Montreal), who wowed newcomers and experienced dancers alike at our May 4 event with a rather unique combination of dances and moves.  No doubt he will have more goodies to share.
