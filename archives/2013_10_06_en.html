Pete's Posse
Fiddler Pete Sutherland has decades of concert touring, teaching, and studio production behind him, and still loves playing for dances.
Oliver Scanlon, a high-school student, has absorbed much of his mentor Pete's phrasing and dynamics.
Add the unstoppable groove of Tristan Henderson's fretted and percussive instruments to their tight twin fiddles (and Oliver's mandolin),and you have a trio to be reckoned with!

Mary Wesley
A dance leader, caller, and organizer for contras, squares, morris, and family and community dances, Mary travels throughout New England and beyond to follow and share her love of dance.

"Mary leads dances with a spark and a smile!"
