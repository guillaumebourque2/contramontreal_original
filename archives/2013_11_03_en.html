Frost and Fire
A relatively new band, made up of four virtuosi folk musicians well known throughout the Northeast.

  Hollis Easter (flute, bagpipes, whistle, guitar, foot percussion, vocals)
  Peter Macfarlane (fiddle, low whistle, vocals)
  Viveka Fox (fiddle, bodhran, djembe)
"If you have the opportunity to hear them play live, don’t miss it!" — Gary Apfel
