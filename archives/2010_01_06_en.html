What's a contradance?
In a contradance, guided by a caller, a couple dance a figures with a series of other couples. It's great fun. Watch a video (on YouTube) of a dance, listen to the documentary (in French) by Stéphane Leclair of Radio-Canada on our November 2008 dance, or read the Globe and Mail piece about one of our dances. More.

I don't know how to dance.
The best way to learn about contradances is to go to one. Beginners are welcome. There will be a half hour of introduction, starting at 6.30, to show you the basic moves and calls.

I don't have a partner.
You don't need one.

I don't have the proper shoes and clothes.
You don't need special shoes or clothes. It does help keep the floor clean if you change from your outdoor shoes to light, indoor shoes.
