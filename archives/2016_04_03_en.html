Special event! Square dance for contradancers with David Millstone and the All Day Breakfast Stringband, 3PM to 5PM
 

Nova
 
  Kathleen Fownes (fiddle)  
  Guillaume Sparrow-Pepin (Accordion and Piano)  
  Everest Witman (Guitar and Feet)
 
Hailing from New England, Nova is a young contra dance band that weaves the sounds of fiddle, accordion, and guitar into a rich tapestry of sound. Nova draws from both traditional repertoire and original compositions to create versatile, high energy and driving dance music.

"I know a big deal is made about how young they are, both as individuals and as a band... but the bottom line for me is that they make really, really good dance music."

— Nils Fredland, contra dance caller and member of Elixir

 

David Millstone
David Millstone is known for his clear and supportive teaching style, the breadth of his repertoire, his sense of humor, and his ability to pick dances that offer the right level of challenge for all audiences. With an American repertoire ranging from traditional New England chestnuts to high-energy recent compositions, he calls contras, squares, and interesting dances in odd formations.
