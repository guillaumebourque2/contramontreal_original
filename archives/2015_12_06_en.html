Special event! Workshop with Sarah VanNorstand, 3:30PM to 5PM, 10$
 

Andrew and Noah VanNorstrand
 
  Andrew VanNorstrand (fiddle, guitar, bouzouki)  
  Noah VanNorstrand (fiddle, mandolin, feet)
 
The VanNorstand brothers are finally coming back to Montreal to grace us with their music and their energy! These multi-instrumentalist geniuses grew up making music together and now criss-cross America, delighting dancers. Deep grooves, energetic performances, creative arrangements, don't miss those amazing musicians!


Sarah VanNorstrand
Sarah, from Syracuse, NY, says: "Above all, my goal as a caller is to make sure the dancers are having as much fun as I am! I love dancing, community and music and I always try to communicate that enthusiasm in my calling." Watch her in action here.
